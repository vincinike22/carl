# Carl prototype pass 2

## What changed
- softened the landing screen and copy to make entry feel easier and less clinical
- introduced warmer, calmer visual treatment inspired by high-trust consumer reflection products
- improved prompt language so it feels more humane without becoming vague or fluffy
- refined summary tone to sound steadier, less mechanical, and less severe
- reframed weekly review and themes language to feel more invitational while preserving discipline

## Why
The first prototype proved the product loop. This pass improves willingness to enter the loop.

The aim was to borrow the accessibility and emotional ease of products like Calm or Headspace without drifting into wellness branding, therapy framing, or symbolic fog.

## What stayed intentionally true to Carl
- private by default posture
- serious, non-clinical tone
- Jung-informed reflection language without mystical certainty
- emphasis on patterns, tension, recurrence, and open questions
- no feature expansion or architecture broadening

## What remains mocked
- transcription
- summary generation intelligence
- recurrence detection intelligence
- export
- durable persistence
