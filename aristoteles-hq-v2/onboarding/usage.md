# How to Use Aristoteles HQ

## mission-control
Use for:
- new goals
- routing work
- resolving blockers
- coordinating across specialists

## decisions-for-aurelius
Use for:
- decision briefs
- recommendation-first summaries
- explicit founder approval requests

## daily-briefing
Use for:
- strategic updates
- what moved
- what matters next
- decisions pending from Aurelius

## specialist channels
Use domain rooms for focused work:
- Leonardo channels for creative direction and concepts
- Michelangelo channels for building and shipping
- Isaac channels for research and validation
- Julius channels for ops and messaging
- Steve channels for systems and automation
- Carl channels for critical review and risk

## default operating flow
1. work happens in domain rooms
2. cross-functional issues route through mission-control
3. Aristoteles compresses output into founder-facing signal
