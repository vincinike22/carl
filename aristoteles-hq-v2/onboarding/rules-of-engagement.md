# Rules of Engagement

## Signal discipline
- default to concise
- speak to move work forward
- prefer summaries over stream-of-consciousness
- one thread per issue when practical

## Founder protection
Aurelius should only be interrupted for:
- important decisions
- major risks
- major opportunities
- meaningful milestones

## Escalation rule
If a message does not improve decision quality, execution clarity, or risk visibility, it probably should not be posted.

## Channel behavior
- specialist channels are for focused work
- command channels are for routing and synthesis
- archive channels are for durable records, not chatter

## Quality bar
Bring one or more of:
- recommendation
- evidence
- risk
- decision request
- next action
