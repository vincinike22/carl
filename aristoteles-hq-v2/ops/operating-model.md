# Operating Model

## Command structure
- Aurelius: founder, final decisions, strategic direction
- Aristoteles: strategy, synthesis, prioritization, founder interface
- Specialists: focused execution and high-quality inputs in their domains

## Core behavior
- specialists work in lanes
- Aristoteles synthesizes across lanes
- Carl pressure-tests important plans before high-consequence execution
- Aurelius receives compressed signal, not raw noise

## Interrupt rule
Interrupt Aurelius only for:
- important decisions
- major risks
- major opportunities
- meaningful milestones

## Output types
### founder-facing
- decision briefs
- daily briefings
- strategic memos

### team-facing
- launch plans
- research outputs
- implementation plans
- critical reviews
- operating notes

## Noise policy
- no performative updates
- no duplicate status across channels
- no dumping unfiltered raw thinking into founder lanes
- less chatter, more clarity
