# Carl — 60 Second Pitch

Carl is a private reflective companion for the moments when your thoughts are not fully formed yet.

Most AI products optimize for speed and answers. But many of the questions people struggle with are not answer problems, they are reflection problems. Questions like: Should I stay or leave? Why do I keep repeating this pattern? What is this feeling actually about?

Carl creates a calm space for that kind of thinking. Users can write freely in Conversation, keep private or shared entries in Journal, inspect patterns over time in Journey, and generate slower synthesis through Reflection letters.

What makes Carl different is that its memory is visible, its privacy boundaries are explicit, and the whole experience is designed to feel like a quiet room rather than a noisy chatbot.

Technically, we built a SwiftUI app, refactored it into a modular architecture with tested core logic, deployed a backend on a VPS, and connected it to OpenRouter so Carl can return reflective psychologically informed responses.

Carl is not trying to be another assistant. It is trying to become a better space to think inside.
