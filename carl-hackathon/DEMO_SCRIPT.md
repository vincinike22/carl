# Carl Demo Script

## Opening
Carl is a quiet room for the thoughts you are not ready to say out loud yet.

## Step 1 — Conversation
Open the Conversation tab.
Type: “I keep circling back to whether I should stay in this role or leave.”
Explain: Carl is not trying to give fast advice. It is trying to surface the deeper structure of the question.

## Step 2 — Journal
Open Journal.
Show the three modes:
- Private
- Shared with Carl
- Attached
Explain: Carl’s memory is permissioned. The user chooses what becomes part of the reflective system.

## Step 3 — Journey
Open Journey.
Explain: Instead of black-box memory, Carl shows a visible timeline of what the user wrote, what Carl noticed, and what themes emerged.

## Step 4 — Reflection
Open Reflection.
Explain: This is where fragmented thoughts become synthesis. Carl can turn a period of writing into a calmer reflection letter.

## Close
Most AI apps are built to answer faster. Carl is built to help people think more honestly.
