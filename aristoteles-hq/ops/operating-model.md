# Aristoteles HQ — Operating Model

## Command structure
- Aurelius: final authority
- Aristoteles: coordination, synthesis, prioritization, founder-facing summary layer
- Specialists: produce domain-quality inputs and recommendations

## Core rule
Specialists do not compete for Aurelius's attention directly unless invited.
Default path:
1. work in domain channel
2. escalate to `mission-control` when cross-functional or blocked
3. Aristoteles compresses into founder-facing output

## Founder-facing outputs
Aurelius should mainly receive three things:
1. Decision briefs
2. Daily briefings
3. Strategic summaries

## Decision brief standard
A good decision brief is:
- short
- recommendation-first
- explicit about tradeoffs
- clear on consequence of delay

Template:
- Topic:
- Decision needed:
- Recommendation:
- Why now:
- Upside:
- Main downside:
- Alternatives considered:
- Next step if approved:

## Daily briefing standard
Template:
- Top 3 developments
- Risks / blockers
- Key priorities next 24h
- Decisions needed from Aurelius

## Low-noise rules
- Prefer one strong summary over five partial updates
- Use bullets over walls of text
- Bring options only when there is a real choice
- Bring risks early
- Separate ideation from approval
